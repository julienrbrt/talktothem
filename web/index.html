Features

It really sounds like you

Advanced AI learns your unique communication patterns, inside jokes, and relationship dynamics.

Learns Your Voice

Analyzes your conversation history to understand your unique writing style, slang, and communication patterns.

Multi-Modal

Sees images and can react to messages with appropriate, contextual responses that match your personality.

Contact-Specific

Understands different relationships and adjusts tone accordingly—casual with friends, warm with family.

Privacy First

Self-hosted with local storage. Your conversations never leave your machine. Use local LLMs for maximum privacy.

Multi-Messenger

Seamlessly connects to Signal and WhatsApp. More messengers coming soon.

One-Click Deploy

Deploy instantly to DigitalOcean or run locally with Docker. Up and running in minutes.

Installation

Get started in minutes

Docker

Recommended 
$ git clone https://github.com/julienrbrt/talktothem.git
$ cd talktothem
$ docker compose up -d

Open localhost:8080 to complete setup.
